Our Goal
Millioti's existence is a promise. It promises players a nurturing home founded on clear purpose and vision. Believing on our purpose and adhering to it so as to fulfill that promise, exemplified in our motto Learn, Grow, and Evolving Together, is what binds each and every Millioti member together. Hence, Millioti is a brotherhood of players with the same mindset and passion and with the same aspirations. We thus make it known to all and state our purpose and vision below:
1. To advocate Millioti as a family and casual guild by creating a fun, social, and dynamic gaming home for all members. To foster long term friendships among members and enhance the online gaming experience by virtue of those friendships.
2. To establish Millioti as a guild of mature players and to show that it is possible for a guild to thrive on the core values of being a family and casual guild.
3. To realize that we might not be the first guild to accomplish something, but we will eventually accomplish all our goals.
4. To provide an opportunity for skilled or well geared members to help those who are not as skilled or geared to advance and learn.
5. To get everyone, not just officers, involved in making Millioti a closely knit, stronger, and prosperous guild.
