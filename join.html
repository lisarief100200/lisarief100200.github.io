How To Join ?
Hello There!
Thank you very much for your interest in joining Millioti. Considering to join Millioti is your first personal step towards a much more exciting Ghost Blade gaming experience. New Millioti brothers and sisters are always welcome and we look forward to having you as our guild mate. We are excited!
There are some things that you must do first and processes to undergo to before you become a member.

Step one: Rank & Criteria. Do you satisfy all of the following requirements?
‣ Minimum Rank: Silver (Level 75 or higher)
‣ Must have cleared Floor 30 of King's Tower
‣ Desire to make Millioti as the primary in-game guild
‣ Willing to always use the Discord app
‣ Must have a Facebook account

Step two: Make it known that you want to join our guild by messaging or mailing in-game any of the guild officers listed below.
• RoyArche
• KnightRYn
• MedusaXsaRe

Step three: Wait to be contacted by a guild recruiter. Your journey toward becoming a Millioti member officially begins once you agree to and follow the instructuions of the guild recruiter.
