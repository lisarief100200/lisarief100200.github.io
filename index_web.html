About Us
Entablised in 2020, Millioti is a Indonesian guild of MMORPG called Ghost Blade in the South East Asia server.
Members of the guild share a common ground: to have fun playing Ghost Blade together. Millioti is more than that however, as members believe and subscribe to the idea of belonging to a collective organization that fosters camaraderie and sincere fellowship. Thus, Millioti, in a deeper sense is a family, a brotherhood.
Join now to begin your journey into becoming one of use and receive a lot of benefit!
- Millioti77 (Guildmaster)
